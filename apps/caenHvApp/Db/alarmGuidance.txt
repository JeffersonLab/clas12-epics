
HIHI = Hardware reports error.  Chceck the HV GUI for channel status.  If tripped, try resetting the channel with the ON/OFF button from the GUI.  If problem persists, contact the detector expert.

LOLO = IOC lost communications with the mainframe.  Try rebooting the IOC.  If problem persists after a few minutes, the HV mainframe may be in need of a reboot.

LO/HI = Mismatch between measured and demand voltage.  Acknowledge the alarm and notify the detector expert for guidance.
