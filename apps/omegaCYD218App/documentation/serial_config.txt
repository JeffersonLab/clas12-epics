Serial Config
-------------
- Uses RS-232C
- 9600 max baud
- Character bits: 1 Start, 7 Data, 1 Parity (odd), 1 Stop
- Terminators: CRLF
- 20 commands per second max
- ";" seperator for commands
- typically 10ms response time.  some are longer.
- FOR INTERMITTENT LOCKUPS increase delay to 100ms to prevent overloading.
